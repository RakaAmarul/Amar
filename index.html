Deloitte was founded by William Welch Deloitte in 1845 in London, originally as an auditing firm. After several mergers, including with Touche Ross in 1989, it became Deloitte & Touche.

Today, Deloitte is one of the world's largest professional services firms, offering audit, consulting, tax, and risk management services. Its main focus is on technology innovation and digital transformation, with offices in over 150 countries.

Deloitte's mission is to make an impact that matters by delivering lasting results for clients, people, and communities. It focuses on driving innovation, empowering clients, fostering a diverse workplace, and addressing global challenges like sustainability and social responsibility.

Deloitte's vision is to be the standard of excellence, creating positive impact for clients, people, and society. The firm aims to be the most trusted and respected professional services organization, driving innovation and leadership in areas like technology, sustainability, and business transformation.
